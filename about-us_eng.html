  • Family company with traditions

    Aerosol Service is a company with 30 years of experience in contract manufacturing and private label in the cosmetics and household chemicals sectors. Our passion and commitment to creating products make us highly valued by our business partners. We are proud that our high-quality products are appreciated by many customers, whose products can be found on store shelves worldwide. We constantly strive to improve our services and create innovative solutions that have a real impact on the quality of life for consumers around the world.
