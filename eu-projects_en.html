  • Beneficiary: Aerosol Service Spółka z ograniczoną odpowiedzialnością

  • Project title: "Strengthening the potential and improving the eco-efficiency of Aerosol Service sp. z o.o. through the purchase of a production line for filling technical aerosols" Implementation period: 16-MAR-2017 - 31-DEC-2021

  • Value of the project: 2,460,000.00 PLN

  • European Funds contribution: 798,000.00 PLN

  • Project objective:

  • The main objective of the project is to increase Aerosol Service's competitiveness in the market of aerosol manufacturers, primarily technical aerosols in the European context, reduce the raw material and energy intensity of production processes, and introduce improved and new services to the company's offer. Among the specific objectives are: Enabling the implementation of R&D results on the use of compressed gases, increasing the market share of green aerosols, and reducing the level of unemployment in the region.

  • Planned outcomes:
  • The effect of the project will be to improve the eco-efficiency of production at Aerosol Service, i.e. reducing the raw material and energy intensity of production processes and increasing the Beneficiary's export by introducing new, market-desired services in the European market, such as packaging production and filling of technical aerosols.

  • Implementation period: 15-APR-2019- 31-DEC-2021

  • Value of the project: PLN 423,776.00

  • Project objective:
  • The objective of the project is to promote the Aerosol Service brand on international markets, which will translatę directly into the level of export sales revenues obtained by the Beneficiary. Within the framework of realization of the project objective, the Beneficiary will participate in 3 trade fair and exhibition events as an exhibitor, in 4 trade fair and exhibition events as a participant, as well as in an economic mission to the Russian Federation and organize inbound missions.
